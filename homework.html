About Me

Hola, my name is Thomas Urdinola! I am currently a third year Computer Science student with a Cybersecurity minor at the University of Maryland: College Park. A couple of cool things about me is that my parents are Colombian, so spanish was my first language growing up. Last summer, I had the opportunity to be a Software Engineer Intern at Freddie Mac, where we mainly focused on moving data to a cloud-based database system. This upcoming summer however, I am proud to announce that I'll be a Software Engineer intern for Microsoft! A dream come true :)

Some of my interests include: Programming Languages, Algorithms, Quantum Computing, and ML/AI. Nevertheless, I am always intersted in learning new things in CS. In school, I am a bootcamp member in the App Dev club and executive team lead for the College Success Scholars program, a Black and Latino male program that helps students of that race stay on track with their academic and professional skills. Additionally, I am a Teaching Assistant for CMSC330: Organization of Programming Languages, I like OCaml a lot hehe

Apart from academics, I love to watch/play soccer, with FC Barcelona being my favorite team. In addition, I love to go thrifting, and listening to music. \ Thank you so much for taking the time in getting to know me!!
